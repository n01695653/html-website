1. IDLI-SAMBHAR

The ingridents used to make Idli Sambhar.

The Instructuion To Follow To Make Recipe .

  1. Idli
  2. Soak: Soak 1 cup urad dal and 2 cups idli rice (with 1 tsp fenugreek seeds if desired) for 6-8 hours.
  3. Grind: Blend soaked ingredients with a little water until smooth.
  4. Ferment: Mix, add salt, and let it ferment for 8-12 hours.
  5. Steam: Grease idli molds, fill with batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Sambhar
  7. Cook Dal: Cook 1 cup toor dal in a pressure cooker with 3 cups water until soft.
  8. Prepare Veggies: Chop mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, etc.).
  9. Cook Veggies: Boil veggies with water and 1-2 tbsp tamarind paste until tender.
  10. Combine: Add cooked dal and 1-2 tbsp sambhar powder; simmer for 10 minutes.
  11. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and dried red chilies. Pour over sambhar.
  12. Serve
  13. Enjoy idlis with sambhar and coconut chutney!
