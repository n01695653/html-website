2. PANEER TIKKA

The ingridents used to make Paneer Tikka .

The Instructuion To Follow To Make Recipe .

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, chaat masala, salt, and oil.
  2. Coat paneer, bell peppers, and onions in this mixture.
  3. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. COOK:
  5. Skewer the marinated paneer and vegetables.
  6. Grill or bake in an oven at 200°C (400°F) for 12-15 minutes until lightly charred.
  7. Serve:
  8. Sprinkle with chaat masala and serve hot with lemon wedges and chutney.
